Money Splitter Using C++

The Money Splitter project, developed in C++, efficiently manages group expenses by dividing costs among participants. Utilizing advanced data structures like linked lists, queues, stacks, and trees, the program tracks individual contributions, calculates each person's share, and determines the amount owed or to be refunded. This ensures a fair and transparent allocation of expenses, simplifying financial management for group activities.
